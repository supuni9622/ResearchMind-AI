# Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)

## Purpose

Architecture Decision Records capture important engineering decisions made during the development of ResearchMind AI.

Each ADR answers three questions:

- What problem were we solving?
- What decision was made?
- What are the consequences?

ADRs preserve architectural reasoning for future contributors.
